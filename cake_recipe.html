recipe

chocolate cake

Here is a fast and easy version of the classic chocolate cake recipe. Enjoy!

Difficulty Preparation cooking total time
easy 25 minutes 10 minutes 35 minutes

Ingredients


Dark chocolate: 200g

Butter: 200g

sugar: 150g

Flour: 80g

Eggs: 4

Instructions

1

 melt the cholcolate and the butter together

2

 mix the sugar, eggs and the flour

3

 mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

bake in the oven
